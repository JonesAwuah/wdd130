Whitewater Adventures

Our Mission and Goal

At Whitewater Adventures, our mission is to provide exhilarating rafting experiences for all adventurers, young and old. We believe in pushing boundaries, embracing nature, and creating unforgettable memories on every river. Our goal is to bring the thrill of whitewater to the world, safely and responsibly, while fostering a love for outdoor exploration. Join us as we embark on the adventure of a lifetime!

History

Founded in the wild waters of the Crooked River in 1985, Whitewater Adventures began as a small group of river enthusiasts with a single raft and a big dream. Over the years, we’ve grown from a handful of adventurers to a world-renowned rafting company, offering thrilling rides on rivers across the globe.

Our motto, "Paddle Hard, Laugh Harder," was born from our first wild ride where nobody stayed dry — but everyone had a blast! From humble beginnings to global adventures, we’re committed to making every rafting trip as unforgettable as our very first one.
